Welcome to IYEPA

Who We Are

Presently, Micro, Small to Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) are the backbone of the Kenyan economy yet they continue to grapple for business growth and sustainability. Alternatives Africa (Alternatives) has therefore established the Inter-County Young Entrepreneurs and Professionals Association (IYEPA) as the Affordable Ecosystem Affiliate that identifies and addresses the marketing and policy challenges of young entrepreneurs and professionals.

Briefly, Alternatives Africa (Alternatives) is a youth-founded Non-profit Social Enterprise (Company Limited by Guarantee) that focuses on youth socioeconomic development in both rural and urban areas. Through a trichotomic model that includes a Cooperative Society and a Business Association, Alternatives has designed an Affordable Ecosystem that offers both financial and non-financial support services to youth-led micro and small enterprises. The objective is to accelerate business profitability and growth, improve individual economic livelihoods as well as create employment for other unemployed youth (youth-led job creation), with the end goal of addressing the youth unemployment challenge.

IYEPA (Member of the Kenya Private Sector Alliance-KEPSA) therefore exists as the business association to support a diverse range of micro and small enterprises run by entrepreneurs and professionals aged between 18-40 years. Having both the entrepreneur and the enterprise in mind, it is dedicated to playing a pivotal role in creating a robust business ecosystem that is favourable to the success of young entrepreneurs and professionals. IYEPA is therefore founded on a holistic framework that includes a variety of effective activities and services tailored to provide non-financial support services particularly marketing services, access to new and existing markets as well as participation in both public and private sector policy and decision making processes.

our community goals

  • Provide a unified voice for young entrepreneurs and professionals, guided by membership insights.
  • Foster a climate for investment in youth-owned MSMEs, supporting both profit and impact.
  • Create a platform for engagement and collaboration with multi-sectoral stakeholders through inclusive business frameworks.
  • Offer a platform for information exchange, affordable marketing, and advocacy for youth-friendly policies, legislation, and regulation.
  • Develop mutually beneficial relationships through networking and trade in a supportive enterprise ecosystem.

our extended community

IYEPA - Inter-County Young Entrepreneurs and Professionals Association

IYEPA is committed to supporting the growth and development of youth-led micro and small enterprises, particularly in the agribusiness sector, to create a favorable environment for their success. The following objectives guide IYEPA's efforts:

  • Promote a positive perception of agriculture that appeals to the youth;
  • Provide affordable financial and non-financial business support services (including training and access to markets) across the agricultural value chain;
  • Facilitate access to modern agricultural equipment and ICT for innovative, home-grown solutions that are profitable and competitive;
  • Encourage value addition and the establishment of cottage industries based on Kenya's comparative advantages in its forty-seven (47) Counties;
  • Organize inter-county and inter-country exchange programs for exposure and knowledge sharing in the agricultural and other relevant sectors.
Alternatives Africa

Alternatives Africa, founded in 2014, is a non-profit focused on empowering young entrepreneurs in both rural and urban areas. The organization provides a comprehensive ecosystem for youth-owned micro and small enterprises, offering affordable financial and business support, access to capital, and tailored development programs.

Key platforms within this ecosystem include:

  • Mimi na Wewe Youth Savings and Credit Cooperative (MWY SACCO) - 2015
  • Inter-County Young Entrepreneurs and Professionals’ Association (IYEPA) - 2016
  • Alternatives Agrohub - 2015
  • Youth Economic Dialogues (YED) - 2015
  • Alternatives Socio-economic Enterprise Development Fund (A SEED) - 2022

Agro Sector Milestones and Initiatives

Alternatives Africa has made significant strides in the agricultural sector through the **Alternatives Agrohub**, supporting youth-led enterprises and creating employment. Key achievements include:

  • Representation in the **ASNET-led CAP-F** under the African Union's CAADP program.
  • Facilitating the **Farmpreneur Programme**, focusing on the onion value-chain.
  • Hosting the first **Indo-Kenya 3D Virtual Agro Expo** with 600 global participants.

Alternatives continues to advocate for youth-friendly policies, including:

  • Partnering with **KEPSA** to create youth-focused agriculture policies.
  • Promoting **agro-processing and value addition** in mango, potato, and onion sectors.
  • Engaging with **global stakeholders** to foster innovation and partnerships in the agro-sector.
